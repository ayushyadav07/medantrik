Our History...

Medantrik Medtech, born out of the challenges faced during the 2021 oxygen shortage crisis, is a medical startup driven by a mission to elevate healthcare outcomes through technology and data analytics integration. Originating from informative YouTube videos and a WhatsApp group named Oxygen Man, the initiative evolved into an open-source platform, uniting global engineers to develop low-cost oxygen concentrators.

Inspired by the vision of turning engineers into “Oxygen Man” superheroes, Medantrik Medtech officially launched on June 2, 2022. The startup boasts a team of talented individuals, including experts from prestigious institutions like IITs and seasoned doctors. Their collective dedication centers on improving healthcare accessibility, affordability, and effectiveness, transcending the initial crisis to revolutionize the entire healthcare ecosystem.

Medantrik’s commitment is evident in its pursuit of innovative solutions that not only address immediate challenges but also streamline healthcare operations, enhance patient engagement, and provide valuable insights through data analytics. In essence, Medantrik exemplifies the transformative power of collaboration, innovation, and a resolute commitment to leveraging technology for a positive impact in the healthcare industry.
