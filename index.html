Protein
Excess protein in urine could be a sign of preeclampsia, UTI, or certain other disorders.
Glucose
During pregnancy, it is normal to have some presence of glucose in urine, but elevated levels could indicate gestational diabetes.
Leukocytes
These are White Blood Cells which are usually increased during pregnancy due to physiologic stress. A high level might indicate infection.
Ketones
These are found in urine when the body is low on carbs or dehydrated. If found in combination with sugar, it may indicate gestational diabetes.
Nitrites
Presence of nitrites may indicate bacteria or UTIs during pregnancy.
Blood
Blood in urine during pregnancy could indicate UTI.
